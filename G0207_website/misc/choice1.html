Introduction

In today's digital age, the proliferation of disinformation poses significant challenges in distinguishing between truth and falsehood. Technological advancements, such as deepfakes and sophisticated disinformation techniques, have made it increasingly difficult to discern real information from fake. The sheer volume and speed at which information spreads on digital platforms further compound this challenge. Additionally, cognitive biases, the global reach of disinformation, concerns about freedom of speech, and the lack of digital literacy among individuals contribute to the complexity of the issue.
